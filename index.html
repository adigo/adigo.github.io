Who is Adigo?

In case you wonder, Adigo is just my nickname. My real name is Cheng-Yi Lee. I was born in Taipei County(now became New Taipei City), Taiwan and currently live in Las Cruces, New Mexico in the U.S. Since I am not a native speaker, writing anything in English is a real challenge to me so, please bear with me for my lousy English.

What does "Adigo" mean?

I am actually not sure!

During my childhood, one of my neighbor started calling me A-Di-Go. "A-Di" means "Little Brother" I believe, and "Go" probably means nothing.

There is one culture thing I need to tell you about Taiwan, which is, in every IT companies, people call each other by their Enlish name, therefore, you got to have an English name. So when I got my first job in Taipei as a System Engineer and people asked me what is my English name, The first name came accross my mind is "Adigo"!

After I came here in the States, since nobody can pronounce "Adigo" correctly as it is actually not English, and even worse, nobody can pronounce my real name Cheng-Yi correct either, I started using different names. My co-workers call me Cheng, people in the church call me Jerry, and Starbucks clarks calls me Lee. However, deep inside my heart, I am Adigo!
